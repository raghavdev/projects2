Workforce Utilization Reporting

 

Workforce Utilization Reporting Instructions

  • Workforce utilization reports are worksheets that measure the utilization of minorities and women as employees on state contracts.
  • Any contractors and subcontractors performing work on a state contract must submit a workforce utilization report for prime contracts over $25,000 for services and commodities and prime contracts over $100,000 for construction.
  • Each employee of a contractor performing work on a state contract must be assigned a job title on a workforce utilization report.
  • Construction contracts must be submitted monthly. You must indicate the reporting month on the Workforce Utilization Form.
  • Commodities and services contracts must be submitted quarterly. You must indicate the three month reporting period on the Workforce Utilization Form
  • Use the Prime/Subcontractor Workforce Utilization Form to submit your workforce utilization.
  • Once completed, you must print the form, scan it, and email it to the email address mtaworkforceutilization@mtahq.org.
  • You must enter the contract number and company name in the subject line of the email.
  • Use the  EEO and SOC Classification Guide for description of SOC job title and the SOC job code by EEO 1 job categories.
